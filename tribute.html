Tribute to Dr. José Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda

José Rizal was not just a hero—he was a force of intellect, courage, and passion who awakened the Filipino spirit and ignited a revolution with the power of his pen. A true polymath, Rizal was a doctor, writer, artist, and visionary whose words cut through oppression like lightning. His novels Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo shook the foundations of colonial rule and inspired generations to rise for freedom and dignity. He stood fearless in the face of tyranny, armed not with weapons, but with truth, reason, and an unshakable love for his country. Rizal didn’t just change history—he rewrote it with fire
